I Our Heritage
Our Story

A Living Heritage,
A Thriving Community

The New Zealand Sri Lanka Cultural Foundation was established with a singular vision: to create a lasting home for Sri Lankan culture in Aotearoa New Zealand. Founded by a group of passionate community leaders, the foundation has grown into a respected cultural institution.

We organise events that celebrate Sri Lankan traditions, preserve heritage through digital archives and storytelling, and build bridges between Sri Lankan and New Zealand communities. Our work extends from cultural galas and dance workshops to youth mentorship programs and heritage preservation projects.
